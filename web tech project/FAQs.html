Frequently asked questions

  1. Can I donate unused medicines ?
    Yes, used medicines can be donated if they are not expired
  2. There is some cough syrup left in the bottle which I used for my child, can I donate the same?
    No, We do not accept unsealed bottles of medicines. Please avoid the same.
  3. Can i donate old medical equipments ?
    Sure, give your old medical equipment a new home. Donate to us.
  4. Can I organise a donation camp in my office or housing society?
    Sure, we encourage you to please organise a donation camp in your office, school, college, residential society and with all like minded people
