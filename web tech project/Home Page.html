AIM

In the face of disaster the natural impulse is to reach out and help those in need. Donations of medicines can alleviate people suffering, and relief efforts benefit enormously from donations by private individuals, groups and organizations. The first and paramount principle is that a donation should be intended only to assist the recipient, and all efforts should be made to maximise its positive effects.

Sometimes when you are dealing with a serious problem you watch the world wandering happily by and want to either a) cry b) drown your sorrows c) say some rude words or d) all of the above, because it seems you are the only one in this sorry situation. Well, you are not. Although there will be ups and downs you will come out the other side.

So, we aim to donate and help people as much as we can.
